Kinect Planograms

I worked with the Microsoft Kinect to design and build a working prototype as a tech demo. My goals were to research and explore interactions in 3d space, while being able to demo them in real time.

We were pitching a client a small glimpse of what the future of store building and analytics could look like and rapidly demoing tech to show that we were thinking about the future of interaction design.
