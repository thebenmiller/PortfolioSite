Vice: Munchies | City Guides

Exploring Your City with Munchies

The Vice Munchies brand needed a place for people to explore a curated list of Munchies' favorite places in cities throughout the USA.

Go to Cityguides.munchies.tv

As the UX lead on the project, I worked with a team of stakeholders at Munchies to concept what a city guide could look like inside the already established guidelines of the Munchies web experience. I went from concept to completion in a short timeframe, working with support from a visual designer, and our in house development team.

My goal of the project was to create an experience that focuses on quick comparison and to give the Munchies team an easy way to build in quick in-roads to Munchies.com articles and content. The Munchies team has since sucessfully launched 8 city guides.
